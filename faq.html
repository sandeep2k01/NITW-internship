Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

What is the Scholarship Portal?

The Scholarship Portal is a platform providing information about various scholarships available for students.

How can I apply for a scholarship?

Each scholarship has its own application process. Please refer to the details provided under each scholarship type for specific instructions.

Who is eligible for scholarships?

Eligibility criteria vary for different scholarships. Please check the individual scholarship details for more information.

How do I know if I have been awarded a scholarship?

You will be notified via email or through the scholarship portal if you have been awarded a scholarship.
