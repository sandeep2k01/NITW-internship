Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

What is the Scholarship Portal?

The Scholarship Portal is a centralized platform providing information about various scholarships available for students across India and internationally.

How can I apply for a scholarship?

Each scholarship has its own application process. Please navigate to the relevant scholarship type (International, National, State, or Private) and click "Go to Portal" to visit the official scholarship website.

Who is eligible for scholarships?

Eligibility criteria vary for different scholarships based on category (SC/ST/OBC/General), income, academic merit, state of domicile, and course of study. Please check the individual scholarship portal for specific eligibility requirements.

How do I know if I have been awarded a scholarship?

You will be notified via SMS or email registered on the scholarship portal. You can also check your application status using the Scholarship Status page on this portal.

What is One Time Registration (OTR)?

OTR is a unique registration number allotted to students on the National Scholarship Portal (NSP). It is mandatory for applying for any scholarship from AY 2024-25 onwards and can be generated using your Aadhaar number.

What documents are required for scholarship applications?

Common documents include Aadhaar card, income certificate, caste certificate (if applicable), mark sheets, bank account details (linked with Aadhaar), and a bonafide certificate from your institution.
