About Our Scholarship Program

Welcome to NASS, the premier platform for scholarship assistance. Our commitment lies in assisting students from diverse backgrounds in securing the financial assistance necessary to pursue their educational aspirations. Our repository encompasses a diverse array of scholarships, spanning national, international, state-based, and private funding sources, ensuring that every student can discover the ideal financial support for their academic pursuits.

Time Line

Role of Students:

Submission of Application with relevant Documents at college with 15 days of Admission. Aadhar biometric Authentication at Meeseva.

Role of College/Prinipal:

Verify the students application and forward to biometric authentication Attendance Local or Non Local Bonafide Discontinued/Regular Submission of Signed Barcode hardcopy at district office with Relevant Documents Submission of Utilization certificates of sanctioned students to district Welfare officer within 15 days of RTF credited,

District Officer:

Sanctioned and release of MTF and RTF .

The Portal is open for One Time Registration (OTR) for students. Apply Now!

One Time Registration (OTR) is a unique number allotted to the student and is applicable for the entire academic career on National Scholarship Portal.

OTR is required to apply for the scholarships on National Scholarship Portal from AY 2024-25.

OTR can be generated using Aadhaar/Aadhaar Enrollment number (EID)
